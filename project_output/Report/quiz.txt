REVISION QUIZ — IoT Device
============================================================

Q1. What was the first IoT device and what did it do?
   A) A smart thermostat that regulated temperature.
   B) A modified toaster that could be controlled over the internet.
   C) A security camera that sent alerts to users.
   D) A fitness tracker that monitored physical activity.
   ✅ Answer: B
   💡 The first IoT device was a modified toaster that could be controlled over the internet, marking the beginning of the IoT era.

Q2. Who coined the term 'Internet of Things' and in what year?
   A) Tim Berners-Lee, 1995
   B) Kevin Ashton, 1999
   C) Mark Zuckerberg, 2004
   D) Elon Musk, 2010
   ✅ Answer: B
   💡 Kevin Ashton coined the term 'Internet of Things' in 1999 to describe a system where the internet connects to the physical world.

Q3. Which of the following is NOT a communication protocol commonly used by IoT devices?
   A) Wi-Fi
   B) Bluetooth
   C) Zigbee
   D) HTTP/2
   ✅ Answer: D
   💡 HTTP/2 is primarily a protocol for transferring web content, whereas Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and Zigbee are specifically designed for device-to-device communication in IoT.

Q4. What technological advancements have contributed to the growth of IoT devices?
   A) Only advancements in hardware.
   B) Advancements in wireless communication, sensor technology, and cloud computing.
   C) Only improvements in software development.
   D) Only the rise of social media.
   ✅ Answer: B
   💡 The rapid proliferation of IoT devices is attributed to advancements in wireless communication, sensor technology, and cloud computing.

Q5. In which sectors have IoT devices seen significant adoption?
   A) Only in smart homes.
   B) Healthcare, agriculture, and industrial automation.
   C) Only in entertainment.
   D) Only in manufacturing.
   ✅ Answer: B
   💡 IoT devices have been adopted across various sectors, including healthcare, agriculture, and industrial automation, showcasing their versatility.
